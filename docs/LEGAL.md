# Raimpage Legal Framework

## Overview

Raimpage is an AI music streaming platform. This document outlines the legal requirements and protections needed for launch.

---

## Priority 1: Protecting the Platform

### DMCA Safe Harbor Requirements

1. **Register a DMCA agent** with the US Copyright Office
2. **Post a clear DMCA takedown policy** on the site
3. **Respond promptly** to valid takedown requests
4. **Implement a repeat infringer policy** (ban users who repeatedly violate)
5. **Don't have actual knowledge** of infringement and act expeditiously when notified

### Terms of Service Must Include

- Users grant Raimpage license to host/stream their content
- Users warrant they own or have rights to upload
- Indemnification clause (users cover legal costs if their content causes issues)
- Limitation of liability
- Right to remove content at discretion
- Dispute resolution (arbitration preferred)

### Business Entity

- **LLC at minimum** for liability protection
- **Delaware incorporation** recommended for legal flexibility

### Insurance

- Cyber liability insurance
- Errors & Omissions (E&O)
- Media liability insurance (covers content-related claims)

---

## Priority 2: Protecting Users

### Privacy Compliance

- **CCPA (California)** - need opt-out for data sales, disclosure of data collected
- **GDPR (EU users)** - consent required, right to deletion, data portability
- **Privacy Policy** must list: data collected, how it's used, third parties, retention, user rights

### Content Ownership

- Users retain ownership of their content
- Raimpage gets a license to host/stream (non-exclusive)
- Clear in ToS what rights users keep vs grant

---

## Priority 3: Helping Creators Protect Their Work

### AI Copyright Landscape (as of March 2026)

| Content Type | Copyrightable? |
|--------------|---------------|
| Fully AI-generated (no human input) | ❌ No |
| Human-written lyrics | ✅ Yes |
| AI music with significant human creative direction | ⚠️ Maybe (gray area) |

### "Meaningful Human Authorship" Standard

- Document the creative process
- Keep records of prompts, edits, arrangements
- Human must make creative decisions, not just push a button

### How Raimpage Helps Creators

- **Timestamp uploads** (proof of creation date)
- Let them declare what's human vs AI in their upload
- Offer licensing options (All Rights Reserved, Creative Commons, etc.)
- Educate them on copyright registration ($65-85 per work)

---

## Priority 4: Monetization

### Payment Processing

- Use **Stripe Connect** (handles payouts to creators, we take a cut)
- Stripe handles most compliance
- Using a payment processor like Stripe usually exempts from money transmitter license requirements

### Tax Requirements

- Issue **1099s** to creators earning $600+ annually
- Collect **W-9s** from US creators
- **W-8BEN** for international creators

### Platform Cut

| Platform | Cut |
|----------|-----|
| Patreon | 5-12% |
| Bandcamp | 15% |
| Twitch | 50% (subs) |
| **Raimpage** | **10-15%** |

## Red Flags / Major Risks

### 🚨 Voice Cloning
If creators use AI to clone real artists' voices, Raimpage could face lawsuits. **Strict policy required.**

### 🚨 Copyright Resemblance
If AI output sounds too similar to existing songs, that's infringement risk.

### 🚨 Evolving Law
AI copyright law is still developing. What's allowed today might change.
